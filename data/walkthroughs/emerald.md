# Pokémon Emerald Walkthrough

## Badge 0: Starting Out
- **Starter choice**: Treecko, Torchic, or Mudkip from Professor Birch on Route 101.
- Mudkip is widely considered the best — Water/Ground dual type after evolution.
- **Route 101**: Zigzagoon, Wurmple, Poochyena.
- **Route 102**: Ralts (rare, Lv 4). Seedot and Lotad both available (Emerald has both Ruby and Sapphire exclusives in some areas).
- **Emerald differences**: Both Team Aqua and Team Magma are antagonists. Battle Frontier post-game. Gym leader rematches. Rayquaza is the featured legendary. Some wild Pokémon locations differ from Ruby/Sapphire. Juan replaces Wallace as 8th gym leader. Wallace is Champion instead of Steven.
- **Petalburg Woods**: Taillow, Shroomish, Slakoth (rare).
- HM requirements: None yet.

## Badge 1: Roxanne (Stone Badge)
- **Gym**: Rustboro City. Rock-type specialist.
- **Roxanne's team**: Geodude (Lv 12), Geodude (Lv 12), Nosepass (Lv 15).
- **Recommended level**: 12-15.
- **Key threats**: Nosepass with Rock Tomb.
- **Strategy**: Water, Grass, Fighting. Mudkip, Lotad, or Shroomish.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Cut outside battle.
- Rescue Devon Goods from Team Aqua in Rusturf Tunnel.

## Badge 2: Brawly (Knuckle Badge)
- **Granite Cave**: Zubat, Geodude, Makuhita, Aron. Deliver letter to Steven for HM05 Flash.
- **Gym**: Dewford Town. Fighting-type specialist.
- **Brawly's team**: Machop (Lv 16), Meditite (Lv 16), Makuhita (Lv 19).
- **Recommended level**: 16-19.
- **Key threats**: Makuhita with Bulk Up + Vital Throw.
- **Strategy**: Flying or Psychic. Taillow and Ralts are effective.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Flash. Pokémon up to Lv 30 obey.

## Badge 3: Wattson (Dynamo Badge)
- **Slateport City**: Deliver Devon Goods. Battle Team Aqua at Museum.
- **Route 110**: Rival battle. Electrike, Gulpin, Plusle, Minun.
- **Gym**: Mauville City. Electric-type specialist.
- **Wattson's team**: Voltorb (Lv 20), Electrike (Lv 20), Magneton (Lv 22), Manectric (Lv 24).
- **Recommended level**: 22-24.
- **Key threats**: Magneton (Steel/Electric). Manectric with Shock Wave.
- **Strategy**: Ground moves. Marshtomp is ideal — Water/Ground is immune to Electric.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Rock Smash outside battle.
- After gym: New Mauville side quest (Wattson gives key).

## Badge 4: Flannery (Heat Badge)
- **Mt. Chimney**: Battle Team Magma. Defeat Maxie.
- **Gym**: Lavaridge Town. Fire-type specialist.
- **Flannery's team**: Numel (Lv 24), Slugma (Lv 24), Camerupt (Lv 26), Torkoal (Lv 29).
- **Recommended level**: 26-29.
- **Key threats**: Torkoal with Overheat.
- **Strategy**: Water, Ground, or Rock. Marshtomp/Swampert excel.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Strength outside battle.
- Get Go-Goggles. Return to Route 111 desert for Trapinch and Sandshrew.

## Badge 5: Norman (Balance Badge)
- **Gym**: Petalburg City. Dad's gym. Normal-type specialist.
- **Norman's team**: Spinda (Lv 27), Vigoroth (Lv 27), Linoone (Lv 29), Slaking (Lv 31).
- **Recommended level**: 28-31.
- **Key threats**: Slaking — enormous Attack, but Truant skips every other turn. Vigoroth has no Truant.
- **Strategy**: Fighting moves. Breloom (Mach Punch) is excellent. Ghost-types immune to Normal STAB.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Surf outside battle.
- Get HM03 Surf from Wally's father.

## Badge 6: Winona (Feather Badge)
- **Weather Institute**: Battle Team Aqua. Get Castform.
- **Route 119-120**: Absol (rare), Tropius, Kecleon (Devon Scope).
- **Gym**: Fortree City. Flying-type specialist.
- **Winona's team**: Swellow (Lv 29), Pelipper (Lv 30), Skarmory (Lv 31), Tropius (Lv 29), Altaria (Lv 33).
- **Recommended level**: 30-33.
- **Note**: Emerald replaces Swablu with Swellow in Winona's team.
- **Key threats**: Altaria with Dragon Dance + Earthquake. Skarmory is extremely bulky.
- **Strategy**: Electric and Ice moves. Manectric or Magneton with Thunderbolt. Ice Beam for Altaria.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Fly outside battle.

## Badge 7: Tate & Liza (Mind Badge)
- **Mt. Pyre**: Both teams active. Team Aqua steals Blue Orb, Team Magma steals Red Orb.
- **Team Magma Hideout** on Jagged Pass, then **Team Aqua Hideout** in Lilycove.
- **Mossdeep Space Center**: Team Magma attacks.
- **Gym**: Mossdeep City. Psychic-type Double Battle.
- **Tate & Liza's team**: Claydol (Lv 41), Xatu (Lv 41), Lunatone (Lv 42), Solrock (Lv 42).
- **Recommended level**: 40-42.
- **Key threats**: Double battle. Solar Beam + Sunny Day combo.
- **Strategy**: Dark, Water, Ghost, or Bug. Sharpedo, Absol, or Crawdaunt. Swampert Surf hits both.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Dive outside battle.

## Badge 8: Juan (Rain Badge)
- **Seafloor Cavern**: Dive from Route 128. Both Groudon and Kyogre awaken.
- **Sootopolis City**: Groudon vs. Kyogre standoff. Go to Sky Pillar (Route 131) to awaken Rayquaza. Rayquaza quells the conflict. Catch Rayquaza later at Sky Pillar.
- **Note**: Emerald has Juan as 8th gym leader, not Wallace. Wallace is the Champion.
- **Gym**: Sootopolis City. Water-type specialist. Ice tile puzzle.
- **Juan's team**: Luvdisc (Lv 41), Whiscash (Lv 41), Sealeo (Lv 43), Crawdaunt (Lv 43), Kingdra (Lv 46).
- **Recommended level**: 43-46.
- **Key threats**: Kingdra (Water/Dragon) has only one weakness: Dragon-type moves. Very bulky with Rest.
- **Strategy**: Grass for most of team. Dragon moves for Kingdra (or just overpower it). Breloom for Whiscash (4× Grass). Manectric for Sealeo and Luvdisc.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Waterfall outside battle.

## Elite Four
- **Victory Road**: Need Surf, Strength, Rock Smash, Flash, Waterfall.
- **Recommended level**: 55-60+.
- **Sidney** (Dark): Mightyena (Lv 46), Shiftry (Lv 48), Cacturne (Lv 46), Crawdaunt (Lv 48), Absol (Lv 49). Fighting or Bug moves.
- **Phoebe** (Ghost): Dusclops (Lv 48), Banette (Lv 49), Sableye (Lv 50), Banette (Lv 49), Dusclops (Lv 51). Dark moves. Sableye has no weaknesses in Gen 3.
- **Glacia** (Ice): Glalie (Lv 50), Sealeo (Lv 50), Sealeo (Lv 52), Glalie (Lv 52), Walrein (Lv 53). Fighting, Fire, or Rock.
- **Drake** (Dragon): Shelgon (Lv 52), Altaria (Lv 54), Flygon (Lv 53), Flygon (Lv 53), Salamence (Lv 55). Ice moves sweep. Ice Beam on Walrein can carry over.
- **Champion Wallace** (Water): Wailord (Lv 57), Tentacruel (Lv 55), Ludicolo (Lv 56), Whiscash (Lv 56), Gyarados (Lv 56), Milotic (Lv 58). Electric and Grass. Milotic is the hardest — high Sp.Def + Recover. Manectric + Breloom cover most.
