## Badge 0: Starting Out
- **Starter**: Bulbasaur / Charmander / Squirtle — Pallet Town, Professor Oak.
- Bulbasaur dominates Brock + Misty. Charmander struggles early. Squirtle balanced.
- **FireRed exclusives**: Ekans (Rt 4, 9-11), Growlithe (Rt 7-8, Pokémon Mansion), Oddish (Rt 5-6, 12-15, 24-25), Scyther (Safari Zone), Electabuzz (Power Plant), Shellder (Rt 19-20 Super Rod).

### Items & TMs
| Location | Items |
|---|---|
| Pallet Town | Potion (from PC) |
| Route 1 | Potion (hidden) |
| Viridian City | Potion (from Mart clerk after Parcel delivery) |
| Viridian Forest | Potion, Antidote, Poké Ball (hidden), Potion (hidden) |

### Key Items
| Item | Given By | Location | Prerequisite |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pokédex + 5 Poké Balls | Professor Oak | Pallet Town lab | Deliver Oak's Parcel from Viridian Mart |

### Unlocks
- Deliver Oak's Parcel (Viridian Mart → Oak) → Pokédex + 5 Poké Balls.
- Pokédex required to buy Poké Balls at any Mart.
- Route 22 rival fight optional pre-Brock XP.

### Gates
- **Route 3**: Blocked by gate NPC until Boulder Badge obtained — Mt. Moon and Cerulean City unreachable.
- **Viridian Gym**: No leader present; locked until all 7 other badges are obtained.
- **Route 22 west dead-end**: Open for early rival XP only; no passage beyond until all 8 badges (Victory Road).

### Side Quests
- **Oak's Aide — Route 2 gate (after Cut)**: Catch 10 Pokémon → HM05 Flash.

## Badge 1: Brock (Boulder Badge)
- **Gym**: Pewter City. Rock-type.
- **Team**: Geodude (Lv 12), Onix (Lv 14).
- **Rec level**: 12-14.
- **Threats**: Onix — high Def, Rock Tomb (lowers Speed).
- **Strategy**: Water/Grass. Butterfree Confusion. Mankey (Rt 22) Fighting.
- **Badge**: Lv 20 obedience. Flash outside battle.

### Items & TMs

### Unlocks
- Boulder Badge → Flash usable outside battle.
- Route 3 opens after Brock.
- Mt. Moon exit → Route 4 → Cerulean City.

### Gates
- **Return to Pewter City (from Cerulean side)**: No hard lock — Mt. Moon is always traversable on foot in both directions. Fast travel via Fly unavailable until HM02 Fly + Thunder Badge (Badge 3 phase).
- **Pewter Museum back entrance**: Blocked by Cut tree — Old Amber unobtainable until HM01 Cut (Badge 2 era).
- **Cerulean Gym / story events**: Physically reachable after Mt. Moon, but Misty must be beaten to progress further east.

## Elite Four
- **Victory Road**: Requires Surf, Strength, Rock Smash.
- **Rec level**: 55-60+.
- **Lorelei** (Ice): Dewgong (52), Cloyster (51), Slowbro (52), Jynx (54), Lapras (54). → Electric, Fighting.
- **Bruno** (Fighting): Onix (51), Hitmonchan (53), Hitmonlee (53), Onix (54), Machamp (56). → Psychic, Flying.
- **Agatha** (Ghost/Poison): Gengar (54), Golbat (54), Haunter (53), Arbok (56), Gengar (58). → Psychic, Ground. Watch Hypnosis + Dream Eater.
- **Lance** (Dragon): Gyarados (56), Dragonair (54), Dragonair (54), Aerodactyl (58), Dragonite (60). → Ice Beam sweeps.
- **Champion** (Rival): Pidgeot, Alakazam, Rhydon + starter-dependent trio. Diverse coverage needed.

### Post-E4 Unlocks
- Complete E4 → Bill takes you to One Island → Tri-Pass (Islands 1-3).
- National Dex (60+ caught species + beat E4) → Cerulean Cave opens → Mewtwo.
- Rainbow Pass (Islands 4-7) → complete Ruby gem quest on One Island + get National Dex.
- Ruby gem + Sapphire gem → give both to Celio → Network Machine → trade with Ruby/Sapphire/Emerald.

### Gates
- **Cerulean Cave**: Locked until National Dex obtained (60+ species); completing the E4 alone is insufficient.
- **Sevii Islands 4-7**: Locked behind Rainbow Pass — requires Ruby gem delivered to Celio + National Dex.
- **Rocket Warehouse (Five Island)**: Locked until both passwords collected (one from Gideon on Five Island, one from Rocket grunts at Mt. Ember).
- **Dotted Hole (Six Island)**: Requires HM01 Cut on the sealed front door — inaccessible without it.

## Sevii Islands

### Progression
1. Beat E4 → Bill takes you to One Island (Tri-Pass: Islands 1-3).
2. Find Ruby gem (Mt. Ember, One Island) → give to Celio.
3. Get National Dex (60+ species in Kanto Dex) → Celio gives Rainbow Pass (Islands 4-7).
4. Find Sapphire gem (Dotted Hole, Six Island) → Gideon steals it.
5. Clear Rocket Warehouse (Five Island) → recover Sapphire from Gideon.
6. Give Sapphire to Celio → Network Machine complete → Hoenn trading enabled.
