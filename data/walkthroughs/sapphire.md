# Pokémon Sapphire Walkthrough

## Badge 0: Starting Out
- **Starter choice**: Treecko, Torchic, or Mudkip from Professor Birch on Route 101.
- Mudkip is widely considered the best starter — Water/Ground after evolution, only weak to Grass.
- **Route 101**: Zigzagoon, Wurmple, Poochyena.
- **Route 102**: Ralts (rare, Lv 4) — catch early. Lotad (Sapphire exclusive, Water/Grass).
- **Sapphire differences**: Lotad instead of Seedot (Routes 102, 114). Lunatone instead of Solrock. Seviper instead of Zangoose. Team Aqua is the antagonist (vs. Team Magma in Ruby). Kyogre instead of Groudon.
- **Petalburg Woods**: Taillow, Shroomish, Slakoth (rare). Shroomish → Breloom is excellent.
- HM requirements: None yet.

## Badge 1: Roxanne (Stone Badge)
- **Gym**: Rustboro City. Rock-type specialist.
- **Roxanne's team**: Geodude (Lv 12), Geodude (Lv 12), Nosepass (Lv 15).
- **Recommended level**: 12-15.
- **Key threats**: Nosepass with Rock Tomb.
- **Strategy**: Water, Grass, Fighting. Mudkip or Lotad (Sapphire exclusive) excel.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Cut outside battle.
- Rescue Devon Goods from Team Aqua in Rusturf Tunnel.

## Badge 2: Brawly (Knuckle Badge)
- **Granite Cave**: Zubat, Geodude, Makuhita, Aron. Deliver letter to Steven for HM05 Flash.
- **Gym**: Dewford Town. Fighting-type specialist.
- **Brawly's team**: Machop (Lv 16), Meditite (Lv 16), Makuhita (Lv 19).
- **Recommended level**: 16-19.
- **Key threats**: Makuhita with Bulk Up + Vital Throw.
- **Strategy**: Flying or Psychic. Taillow or Ralts work well.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Flash. Pokémon up to Lv 30 obey.

## Badge 3: Wattson (Dynamo Badge)
- **Slateport City**: Deliver Devon Goods. Battle Team Aqua at Museum.
- **Route 110**: Rival battle. Electrike, Gulpin, Plusle, Minun.
- **Gym**: Mauville City. Electric-type specialist.
- **Wattson's team**: Voltorb (Lv 20), Electrike (Lv 20), Magneton (Lv 22), Manectric (Lv 24).
- **Recommended level**: 22-24.
- **Key threats**: Magneton (Steel/Electric). Manectric with Shock Wave.
- **Strategy**: Ground-type moves. Marshtomp is ideal — immune to Electric.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Rock Smash outside battle.

## Badge 4: Flannery (Heat Badge)
- **Mt. Chimney**: Battle Team Aqua (Sapphire). Defeat Archie.
- **Gym**: Lavaridge Town. Fire-type specialist.
- **Flannery's team**: Numel (Lv 24), Slugma (Lv 24), Camerupt (Lv 26), Torkoal (Lv 29).
- **Recommended level**: 26-29.
- **Key threats**: Torkoal with Overheat and Body Slam.
- **Strategy**: Water, Ground, or Rock. Marshtomp/Swampert dominates.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Strength outside battle.
- Get Go-Goggles from rival after gym.

## Badge 5: Norman (Balance Badge)
- **Gym**: Petalburg City. Dad's gym. Normal-type specialist.
- **Norman's team**: Spinda (Lv 27), Vigoroth (Lv 27), Linoone (Lv 29), Slaking (Lv 31).
- **Recommended level**: 28-31.
- **Key threats**: Slaking has enormous Attack but Truant ability. Vigoroth has no Truant.
- **Strategy**: Fighting moves. Breloom (Mach Punch) excels. Ghost-types immune to Normal.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Surf outside battle.

## Badge 6: Winona (Feather Badge)
- **Weather Institute**: Battle Team Aqua. Get Castform.
- **Route 120**: Absol (rare), Kecleon (Devon Scope from Steven).
- **Gym**: Fortree City. Flying-type specialist.
- **Winona's team**: Swablu (Lv 29), Tropius (Lv 29), Pelipper (Lv 30), Skarmory (Lv 31), Altaria (Lv 33).
- **Recommended level**: 30-33.
- **Key threats**: Altaria with Dragon Dance + Earthquake. Skarmory very bulky.
- **Strategy**: Electric and Ice moves. Manectric with Thunderbolt. Rock moves also effective.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Fly outside battle.

## Badge 7: Tate & Liza (Mind Badge)
- **Mt. Pyre**: Team Aqua steals Red Orb.
- **Team Aqua Hideout**: Lilycove City. Navigate underwater base.
- **Gym**: Mossdeep City. Psychic-type Double Battle.
- **Tate & Liza's team**: Claydol (Lv 41), Xatu (Lv 41), Lunatone (Lv 42), Solrock (Lv 42).
- **Recommended level**: 40-42.
- **Key threats**: Double battle with Sunny Day/Solar Beam combo.
- **Strategy**: Dark, Water, Ghost, or Bug moves. Sharpedo or Absol are great. Swampert's Surf hits both.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Dive outside battle.

## Badge 8: Wallace (Rain Badge)
- **Seafloor Cavern**: Dive from Route 128. Battle Team Aqua. Kyogre awakens (Sapphire).
- **Sootopolis City**: Kyogre event. Catch Kyogre at Cave of Origin (Sapphire).
- **Gym**: Sootopolis City. Water-type specialist. Ice tile puzzle.
- **Wallace's team**: Luvdisc (Lv 40), Whiscash (Lv 42), Sealeo (Lv 40), Seaking (Lv 42), Milotic (Lv 43).
- **Recommended level**: 42-45.
- **Key threats**: Milotic with Recover and high Sp.Def. Whiscash is Ground/Water.
- **Strategy**: Electric and Grass. Grass does 4× to Whiscash (but Electric does 0×). Breloom or Sceptile.
- **Badge reward**: Can use Waterfall outside battle.

## Elite Four
- **Victory Road**: Need Surf, Strength, Rock Smash, Flash, Waterfall.
- **Recommended level**: 55-60+.
- **Sidney** (Dark): Mightyena, Shiftry, Cacturne, Crawdaunt, Absol (Lv 46-49). Fighting or Bug moves.
- **Phoebe** (Ghost): Dusclops ×2, Banette ×2, Sableye (Lv 48-51). Dark moves. Sableye has no weaknesses.
- **Glacia** (Ice): Glalie ×2, Sealeo ×2, Walrein (Lv 50-53). Fighting, Fire, or Rock.
- **Drake** (Dragon): Shelgon, Altaria, Flygon ×2, Salamence (Lv 52-55). Ice moves devastate.
- **Champion Steven** (Steel): Skarmory, Claydol, Aggron, Cradily, Armaldo, Metagross (Lv 55-58). Fire, Water, Fighting needed. Metagross with Meteor Mash is the biggest threat.
