# Pokémon Ruby Walkthrough

## Badge 0: Starting Out
- **Starter choice**: Treecko, Torchic, or Mudkip from Professor Birch on Route 101 (save him from Zigzagoon).
- Mudkip is widely considered the best starter — Water/Ground after evolution resists Electric, only weak to Grass.
- Torchic becomes Fire/Fighting Blaziken, excellent for late game. Treecko is fast but has fewer gym advantages.
- **Route 101**: Zigzagoon (Lv 2-3), Wurmple (Lv 2-3), Poochyena (Lv 2-3).
- **Route 102**: Ralts (rare, Lv 4) — catch early, evolves into powerful Gardevoir. Seedot (Ruby exclusive).
- **Route 103**: Rival battle (just starter matchup). Wingull available.
- **Petalburg City**: Dad's gym (Normal-type) — can't challenge until 5th badge.
- **Route 104/Petalburg Woods**: Taillow, Shroomish (excellent catch — Breloom is strong), Slakoth (rare).
- HM requirements: None yet.

## Badge 1: Roxanne (Stone Badge)
- **Gym**: Rustboro City. Rock-type specialist.
- **Roxanne's team**: Geodude (Lv 12), Geodude (Lv 12), Nosepass (Lv 15).
- **Recommended level**: 12-15.
- **Key threats**: Nosepass has Rock Tomb (lowers Speed) and decent bulk.
- **Strategy**: Water, Grass, or Fighting moves. Mudkip or Shroomish (Absorb) with type advantage. Lotad from Route 102 also works.
- **Badge reward**: Can use HM01 Cut outside battle.
- **After badge**: Rescue Devon Goods from Team Aqua grunt in Rusturf Tunnel. Get Devon Goods, Letter, and Poké Nav.

## Badge 2: Brawly (Knuckle Badge)
- **Deliver letter**: To Steven in Granite Cave (Route 106). Get HM05 Flash from hiker inside.
- **Dewford Town**: Accessible via Mr. Briney's boat from Route 104.
- **Granite Cave**: Zubat, Geodude, Makuhita, Aron. Aron is an excellent catch (Steel/Rock, evolves to Aggron).
- **Gym**: Dewford Town. Fighting-type specialist. Dark room puzzle.
- **Brawly's team**: Machop (Lv 16), Meditite (Lv 16), Makuhita (Lv 19).
- **Recommended level**: 16-19.
- **Key threats**: Makuhita has Bulk Up and Vital Throw.
- **Strategy**: Flying or Psychic moves. Taillow/Wingull are effective. Ralts with Confusion.
- **Badge reward**: Can use HM05 Flash. Pokémon up to Lv 30 obey.
- Take Mr. Briney's boat to Slateport City.

## Badge 3: Wattson (Dynamo Badge)
- **Slateport City**: Deliver Devon Goods to Captain Stern. Battle Team Aqua at Museum.
- **Route 110**: Cycling Road. Rival battle. Electrike, Gulpin, Plusle, Minun. Get Mach Bike or Acro Bike from Rydel.
- **Gym**: Mauville City. Electric-type specialist. Switch puzzle.
- **Wattson's team**: Voltorb (Lv 20), Electrike (Lv 20), Magneton (Lv 22), Manectric (Lv 24).
- **Recommended level**: 22-24.
- **Key threats**: Magneton resists many types (Steel/Electric). Manectric has Shock Wave.
- **Strategy**: Ground-type moves wreck this gym. Marshtomp (Mudkip evolution) is ideal — Water/Ground is immune to Electric. Geodude/Graveler also immune.
- **Badge reward**: Can use HM06 Rock Smash outside battle.
- **Side**: Route 117 has Roselia, Illumise/Volbeat. Verdanturf Town has Contest Hall.

## Badge 4: Flannery (Heat Badge)
- **Route 111**: Desert (need Go-Goggles, get after Lavaridge). Trapinch available later — evolves to Flygon.
- **Route 112**: Numel. Cable Car to Mt. Chimney.
- **Mt. Chimney**: Battle Team Magma (Ruby). Defeat Maxie. Get Meteorite.
- **Jagged Pass**: Leads down to Lavaridge Town. Spoink available.
- **Gym**: Lavaridge Town. Fire-type specialist. One-way floor puzzle.
- **Flannery's team**: Numel (Lv 24), Slugma (Lv 24), Camerupt (Lv 26), Torkoal (Lv 29).
- **Recommended level**: 26-29.
- **Key threats**: Torkoal has Overheat (massive damage, lowers own Sp.Atk) and Body Slam.
- **Strategy**: Water, Ground, or Rock moves. Marshtomp/Swampert destroys everything here. Tentacool from surfing routes also works.
- **Badge reward**: Can use HM04 Strength outside battle.
- Get Go-Goggles from rival after gym. Return to Route 111 desert for Trapinch, Sandshrew, Cacnea.

## Badge 5: Norman (Balance Badge)
- **Petalburg City** (revisit): Dad's gym. Normal-type specialist.
- **Gym**: Room system — each room has a different strategy trainer (Speed Room, Defense Room, etc.).
- **Norman's team**: Spinda (Lv 27), Vigoroth (Lv 27), Linoone (Lv 29), Slaking (Lv 31).
- **Recommended level**: 28-31.
- **Key threats**: Slaking has Truant ability (skips every other turn) BUT enormous Attack. Facade at +STAB from Slaking is devastating. Vigoroth has no Truant.
- **Strategy**: Fighting-type moves are super effective. Breloom (Mach Punch) is excellent. Ghost-types are immune to Slaking's Normal moves. Use Slaking's Truant turns to set up or heal.
- **Badge reward**: Can use HM03 Surf outside battle.
- Get HM03 Surf from Wally's father in Petalburg after winning.

## Badge 6: Winona (Feather Badge)
- **Route 118-119**: Surf required. Absol (rare Route 120), Tropius (Route 119), Kecleon (invisible, Devon Scope).
- **Weather Institute**: Battle Team Aqua. Get Castform (Weather Ball Pokémon) after clearing.
- **Route 120**: Steven gives Devon Scope (reveals invisible Kecleon). Absol available (Dark-type, high Attack).
- **Fortree City**: Treehouse city. Kecleon blocks gym entrance — use Devon Scope.
- **Gym**: Fortree City. Flying-type specialist. Rotating gate puzzle.
- **Winona's team**: Swablu (Lv 29), Tropius (Lv 29), Pelipper (Lv 30), Skarmory (Lv 31), Altaria (Lv 33).
- **Recommended level**: 30-33.
- **Key threats**: Altaria has Dragon Dance + Earthquake. Skarmory is very bulky (Steel/Flying).
- **Strategy**: Electric moves for most of the team. Ice moves for Altaria and Tropius. Manectric with Thunderbolt is excellent. Rock moves hit everything.
- **Badge reward**: Can use HM02 Fly outside battle.

## Badge 7: Tate & Liza (Mind Badge)
- **Routes 121-126**: Water routes. Lilycove City has Department Store.
- **Mt. Pyre**: Ghost Pokémon (Shuppet, Duskull). Team Magma steals Blue Orb.
- **Team Magma Hideout**: Jagged Pass entrance. Navigate to steal back submarine intel.
- **Route 124-126**: Dive spots. Get HM08 Dive.
- **Mossdeep City**: Space Center. Steven's house.
- **Gym**: Mossdeep City. Psychic-type specialists. Double Battle gym.
- **Tate & Liza's team**: Claydol (Lv 41), Xatu (Lv 41), Lunatone (Lv 42), Solrock (Lv 42).
- **Recommended level**: 40-42.
- **Key threats**: Double battle — Solrock and Lunatone lead with Sunny Day/Solar Beam combo. Claydol has Earthquake.
- **Strategy**: Dark, Water, Ghost, or Bug moves. Sharpedo (Dark/Water) is great. Absol with Shadow Ball or Crunch. Swampert with Surf hits both opponents.
- **Badge reward**: Can use HM08 Dive outside battle.

## Badge 8: Wallace (Rain Badge)
- **Seafloor Cavern**: Dive from Route 128. Battle Team Magma. Groudon awakens (Ruby). Get HM07 Waterfall.
- **Sootopolis City**: Underwater entrance. Groudon vs. Kyogre event. Catch Groudon (Ruby) at Cave of Origin after event.
- **Gym**: Sootopolis City. Water-type specialist. Ice tile puzzle.
- **Wallace's team**: Luvdisc (Lv 40), Whiscash (Lv 42), Sealeo (Lv 40), Seaking (Lv 42), Milotic (Lv 43).
- **Recommended level**: 42-45.
- **Key threats**: Milotic has Recover, high Sp.Def, and Water Pulse. Very bulky.
- **Strategy**: Electric and Grass moves. Breloom, Manectric, or Sceptile excel. Whiscash is Ground/Water so Grass does 4× damage but is immune to Electric.
- **Badge reward**: Can use HM07 Waterfall outside battle.
- After badge: Route 128 → Ever Grande City → Victory Road.

## Elite Four
- **Victory Road**: Need Surf, Strength, Rock Smash, Flash, Waterfall.
- **Recommended level**: 55-60+.
- **Sidney** (Dark): Mightyena, Shiftry, Cacturne, Crawdaunt, Absol (Lv 46-49). Use Fighting or Bug moves.
- **Phoebe** (Ghost): Dusclops ×2, Banette ×2, Sableye (Lv 48-51). Use Dark moves. Sableye has no weaknesses.
- **Glacia** (Ice): Glalie ×2, Sealeo ×2, Walrein (Lv 50-53). Use Fighting, Fire, or Rock. Walrein is bulky.
- **Drake** (Dragon): Shelgon, Altaria, Flygon, Flygon, Salamence (Lv 52-55). Ice moves are devastating. Ice Beam sweeps.
- **Champion Steven** (Steel): Skarmory, Claydol, Aggron, Cradily, Armaldo, Metagross (Lv 55-58). Diverse team. Fire, Water, and Fighting moves needed. Metagross is extremely dangerous with Meteor Mash.
